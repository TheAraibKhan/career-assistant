Why Formatting Matters

Your resume's visual appearance sends a signal before anyone reads a word. Sloppy formatting suggests carelessness. Poor spacing makes it hard to scan. Bad typography looks outdated. These factors combine to cost you interviews.

Good formatting is invisible. It doesn't distract. It guides the reader's eye. It shows professionalism without effort.

Font and Typography

‚úì Do

  • Use standard fonts: Arial, Calibri, Times New Roman
  • 10-12pt font size for body text
  • 11-14pt for headers
  • Consistent spacing throughout
  • Black text on white background

‚úó Don't

  • Use decorative fonts: Comic Sans, script fonts
  • Mix more than 2 fonts
  • Use colored text (except subtle accent colors)
  • Go below 10pt (hard to read)
  • Use shadows or 3D effects
Pro Tip: If you're unsure, stick with Arial 11pt or Calibri 11pt. They're universally readable, ATS-friendly, and look professional.

Spacing & Layout

‚úì Do

  • 1-inch margins on all sides
  • Single-spaced within sections
  • 1.15 or 1.5 spacing between sections
  • Bullet points for easy scanning
  • Consistent indentation

‚úó Don't

  • Custom margins (breaks ATS parsing)
  • Double-spaced text (looks sparse)
  • Text boxes or columns (ATS can't read them)
  • Headers/footers (excluded from ATS)
  • Excessive whitespace (appears unpolished)
The 1-page rule: Aim for 1 page if you have less than 5 years experience. 2 pages is acceptable for senior roles. Never go beyond 2 pages.

Visual Elements & Color

‚úì Do

  • Use 1-2 subtle accent colors for headers
  • Bold job titles for emphasis
  • Italics for company names (optional but clean)
  • Simple horizontal lines to separate sections
  • Professional grayscale palette

‚úó Don't

  • Use more than 3 colors
  • Add logos or pictures
  • Use borders around sections
  • Include colored backgrounds
  • Add emojis or icons
Best color choices: Navy blue, dark gray, or black for main text. Subtle blue or gray for accents (matching your industry standards).

Professional Polish Checklist

Consistent font throughout (1-2 fonts max)
1-inch margins on all sides
No images, logos, or graphics
Black text on white background
Single-spaced within sections
1 or 2 pages maximum
No text boxes, tables, or columns

No spelling or grammatical errors
