What is Career Readiness?

Career Readiness is our comprehensive metric that scores how prepared your resume is to land interviews. It combines all the factors we check: ATS compatibility, content quality, impact, formatting, relevance, and perceived fit for your target roles.

Your Career Readiness Score is not a judgment. It's a roadmap showing exactly where to improve and what will have the biggest impact.

Immediate Actions to Improve

  • Priority 1: Fix ATS blocking issues (remove tables, images, odd formatting)
  • Priority 2: Add metrics to 3 weakest bullets (increase impact by ~25%)
  • Priority 3: Identify and highlight 5-10 keywords matching your target role
  • Priority 4: Get a friend's feedback on the recruiter's 6-second view
  • Priority 5: Re-submit weekly and track improvements (your score should go up 5-10 points per week)

Tracking Your Progress

Each time you upload a new version of your resume, we score it and show you exactly what improved and what still needs work. Watch your score climb week by week.

Pro tip: Most users find that making one focused improvement (like adding metrics to all bullets) creates a noticeable jump in their score. Then they tackle the next improvement.
