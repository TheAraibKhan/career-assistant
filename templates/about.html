Our Story

CareerAssist was born from a simple observation: talented students and early professionals often struggle to present their skills effectively. Traditional resume feedback is expensive, slow, and inconsistent.

We built CareerAssist to democratize access to professional career guidance. Using advanced AI technology, we provide instant, actionable feedback that helps you stand out in today's competitive job market.
