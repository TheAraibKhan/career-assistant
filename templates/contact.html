Frequently Asked

What data do you collect?

We only ask for what's necessary: your resume for analysis, and basic contact info if you share feedback. We never sell your data.

How long does analysis take?

ATS and career readiness analysis happens instantly. You'll see your results as soon as you upload. Detailed insights are available in your dashboard.

Can I delete my data?

Yes. Go to your profile settings and request account deletion. All your data will be removed within 24 hours.

Is CareerOS really free?

We offer a free tier with all core features. We're building premium plans for advanced features, but the basics will always be free.

How is CareerOS different from other tools?

We focus on what actually matters: ATS compatibility, real career alignment, and actionable feedback. No generic scores. No BS.
